Pasta

Description

Italian pasta (pronounced “PAS-tah”) is a collective name for a category of food made from wheat flour and water, sometimes with egg. The name refers to the resulting dough (pasta also means “dough”) that is rolled out and cut into various shapes. Pasta is cooked in water and served with a sauce. All in all, pasta is one of the most famous, most eaten and certainly the most loved dishes in the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups chicken broth, or more as needed
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ tablespoons dried parsley

Steps

  • Make the meat sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Make the ricotta mixture.
  • Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  • Cover with foil and bake.
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving.
